Understanding UV Index & Color Fade

The UV Index is an international standard measurement of ultraviolet radiation intensity. Higher UV exposure accelerates color fading in outdoor surfaces. Rosehill TPV® granules are engineered with advanced UV stabilizers to maintain vibrant colors even in extreme UV environments.

UV Index Scale

Low (0-2)

Minimal protection needed

Moderate (3-5)

Moderate fade risk

High (6-7)

High fade risk

Very High (8-10)

Very high fade risk

Extreme (11+)

Extreme fade risk

Why TPV® Resists UV Fade

Advanced UV Stabilizers: Our thermoplastic vulcanizate formula includes premium UV inhibitors that protect pigments from photodegradation.

Long-Term Color Retention: Independent testing shows TPV® granules maintain over 90% color retention after 5 years of outdoor exposure in high-UV environments.

Global Proven Performance: From the equatorial regions to temperate climates, our surfaces maintain their vibrant appearance worldwide.
