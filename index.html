Rosehill TPV Granules

Cutting-edge, elastomer-based coloured rubber granules manufactured in the United Kingdom. Our unique, 100% fully cross-linked and non-porous granules are polypropylene-free and non-spherical.

  • Premium virgin polymers - no sulphur, clay fillers, or heavy metals
  • Remarkable thermal stability with no carbon black leaching
  • Maintains suppleness under harsh UV conditions
  • Superior colour consistency across all 21 vibrant colours
  • Enhanced UV stability compared to EPDM and SBR granules
  • Custom colour blending service available
