Custom Colour Blends

Surfaces come alive when multiple colours are expertly blended. From subtle two-tone combinations to dynamic multi-colour patterns, our custom blending service allows architects and designers to create truly unique installations.

Each blend is precisely formulated using percentage ratios of our standard colours, ensuring consistent results and reproducible specifications for your projects.

Warm Tones

Gold

2 parts RH41 Bright Yellow / 1 part RH40 Mustard

Sophisticated golden tone perfect for premium installations and accent areas.

Golden Sunshine

1 part RH41 Bright Yellow / 1 part RH40 Mustard / 1 part RH50 Orange

Vibrant three-colour blend creating warm, energetic surfaces ideal for play areas.

Apricot Orange

1 part RH50 Orange / 1 part RH30 Beige

Soft, approachable orange blend suitable for residential and community spaces.

Pumpkin Orange

2 parts RH50 Orange / 1 part RH40 Mustard

Rich autumn-inspired orange perfect for seasonal themes and warm environments.

Natural Earth Tones

Desert Camouflage

1 part RH30 Beige / 1 part RH31 Cream

Natural sandy blend ideal for landscapes that need to blend with surroundings.

Sand

1 part RH30 Beige / 1 part RH40 Mustard / 1 part RH41 Bright Yellow

Multi-tonal sand effect creating natural, organic-looking surfaces.

Autumn Brown

Rich earth-tone blend perfect for natural play environments and parkland settings.

Granite

Contemporary grey blend mimicking natural stone for modern architectural applications.
