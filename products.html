Rosehill TPV®

Coloured Rubber Granules

Cutting-edge, elastomer-based coloured rubber granules manufactured in the United Kingdom. Our unique, 100% fully cross-linked and non-porous granules are polypropylene-free and non-spherical.

  • Premium virgin polymers - no sulphur, clay fillers, or heavy metals
  • Remarkable thermal stability with no carbon black leaching
  • Maintains suppleness under harsh UV conditions
  • Superior colour consistency across all 21 vibrant colours
  • Enhanced UV stability compared to EPDM and SBR granules
  • Custom colour blending service available
Technical Advantage: Our unique polyurethane chemistry delivers non-porous granules that require less binder during installation, reducing costs whilst maintaining superior performance.

Flexilon®

Polyurethane Binder Range

  • Climate-specific solutions from cold to very warm environments

Technical Specifications

Understanding the technical advantages of our integrated surface system

🧪

Chemical Composition

All Flexilon® binders are MDI-based polyurethane systems that are TDI-free and solvent-free. TPV® granules use premium virgin polymers without sulphur, clay fillers, heavy metals like zinc, or phthalate plasticisers.

🌡️

Performance Range

Viscosity ranges from 230cP (primers) to 6000cP (high-viscosity binders). Temperature performance from -40°C to +70°C. Cure speeds from fast (cool climates) to very slow (hot climates/large areas).

⚗️

Application Rates

Binder usage rates: 12-20% for wet pour installations, 10-12% for paver applications, 6% for compression moulding. Primer coverage: 2-3 m²/kg. Pre-treatment: 1-2% of rubber weight.

📦

Storage & Handling

9-month storage stability when kept cool and dry. Containers must be resealed immediately after use. Cure inhibition occurs when exposed to moisture. Flash point >200°C for safe handling.

🔬

Quality Control

NCO values range from 7.5-16.0% depending on formulation. Specific gravity 1.02-1.1. Every batch tested for viscosity, NCO content, and performance characteristics before release.

🌞

UV Stability

Standard binders may yellow with light colours under UV exposure. Flexilon® 1231 includes UV stabilisers to retard yellowing. For complete UV protection, aliphatic binders are available.
