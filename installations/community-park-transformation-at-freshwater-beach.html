Project Overview

Renovations at the public park in Freshwater Beach, NSW, include an aquatic-themed playground inspired by the area's iconic landscape.

This upgrade provides a delightful and relaxing space for both visitors and the local community to enjoy.

The playground's vibrant themed surface, made with Rosehill TPV® wetpour rubber, offers a perfect solution for longer-lasting protection against trips and falls.

Thanks to its UV resistance, these areas retain their durability and softness for many years after installation, regardless of the local climate.

With 21 available colours, it’s easy to incorporate colourful motifs, patterns, and floor games into community spaces.

You can also mix multiple colours to create unique blends for ultimate customisability.

A big thank you to , Pty Ltd, and Regal Innovations for helping transform this space.
