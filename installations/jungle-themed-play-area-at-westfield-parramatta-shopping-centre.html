Jungle-Themed Play Area At Westfield Parramatta Shopping Centre

Parramatta, Australia
April 15, 2024
playground

Project Overview

Check out the new jungle-themed play area at Westfield Parramatta Shopping Centre, designed to captivate children aged 2-5 with an imaginative and engaging environment.

This space provides not only fun and discovery for the kids but also a relaxing area for parents to unwind.

For safety, the area is equipped with Rosehill TPV® rubber surfacing to prevent slips, trips, and falls.

The surface uses a unique blend of green rubber granules that harmonise with the jungle theme while prioritising the safety of young explorers.

Rosehill TPV® is ideal for both indoor and outdoor settings, offering durability and exceptional UV stability.

This ensures it retains its colour and softness longer, even under harsh conditions.

With 22 available colours, designers can create bespoke patterns and themes to enhance any play space.
