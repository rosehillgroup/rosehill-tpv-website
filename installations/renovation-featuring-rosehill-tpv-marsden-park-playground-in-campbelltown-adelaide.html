Renovation Featuring Rosehill TPV®

Marsden Park Playground in Campbelltown, Adelaide.
July 8, 2024
Playground

Project Overview

The Marsden Park Playground in Campbelltown, Adelaide, has reopened to the public after undergoing renovations. As part of the upgrade, the team at Glooloop Surfacing Pty Ltd installed new softfall surfacing using blends of Rosehill TPV® rubber. These surfaces not only complement the surrounding area but also provide cushioned landings, reducing the impact of common playground accidents.With 22 available colours, creating custom blends for any project is easy, resulting in striking and durable surfaces that maintain their vibrancy and softness even in harsh climates.Thanks to Glooloop Surfacing Pty Ltd and Surface Designs TPV
